SMPS (Switched Mode Power Supply)

Switched Mode Power Supply (SMPS) is a compact and efficient type of power supply commonly found in electronic devices. Unlike traditional linear power supplies, SMPS units utilize a switching mechanism to convert electrical power with minimal energy loss. This design allows SMPS to offer stable output voltages while maintaining a smaller size and higher efficiency. Widely used in applications ranging from consumer electronics to industrial equipment, SMPS units can accommodate a wide input voltage range and provide multiple output voltages as needed. Despite their small size, SMPS units deliver reliable performance and play a vital role in powering modern electronic devices.
